Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 18, 2026

InvoPocket gives you full control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time by following the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open InvoPocket

Launch the InvoPocket app on your device and make sure you are signed in to your account.

2

Go to Settings

Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen to open the settings page.

3

Select "Delete Account and Data"

Scroll down and tap the "Delete Account and Data" option. A confirmation dialog will appear.

4

Confirm Deletion

Tap "Delete" to confirm. The app will download a local backup of your data, then permanently remove all cloud data and sign you out.

What Data Gets Deleted

The following table describes what happens to each type of data when you delete your account.

Data Type Action Retention
Account credentials Deleted Immediate
Business profile Deleted Immediate
Invoices and quotations Deleted Immediate
Client records Deleted Immediate
Cloud backup data Deleted Immediate
Local device data Retained Kept on device until you uninstall the app
Crash and analytics logs Deleted Within 30 days

Important Notes

  • Before deletion, the app automatically saves a copy of your cloud data to your device's local storage so you do not lose your work.
  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your cloud data cannot be recovered.
  • If you only want to sign out without deleting data, use the "Sign Out" option in Settings instead.
  • Local data on your device is not affected by account deletion. To remove local data, uninstall the app.

Cannot Access the App?

If you are unable to access the app and need to request account deletion, you can contact us directly by email. Please include the email address associated with your InvoPocket account in your request.
