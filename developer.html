About the project

Why InvoPocket Exists

Most invoicing apps require internet, charge monthly fees, and store your business data on their servers. InvoPocket takes a different approach. Everything runs locally on your device with AES-256 encryption. No server ever sees your client list, revenue numbers, or invoice history in plain text. Cloud backup is optional and end-to-end encrypted.

The app is built with Flutter, targeting Android first, with a focus on speed, simplicity, and privacy. It is made in Odisha for businesses everywhere.

Built with

Technology Stack

Flutter + Dart

Cross-platform UI framework for native performance on Android and iOS.

SQLite (sqlcipher)

On-device encrypted database for offline-first data storage.

Firebase + Firestore

Optional cloud backup with Google Auth and encrypted sync.

AES-256 Encryption

Military-grade encryption applied to all cloud-synced data before transmission.

Riverpod

Reactive state management for responsive and predictable UI updates.

PDF + Excel Export

Dynamic document generation with GST calculations and UPI QR codes.

Core principles

What Drives This Project

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. Cloud backup is optional and fully encrypted. Zero tracking, zero analytics cookies.

Offline Always

No internet needed for any core function. Create invoices, manage clients, and export reports without connectivity.

Free Forever

No subscriptions, no paywalls, no hidden charges. All features are unlocked from day one.

Made in India

Built from Odisha with a deep understanding of how small businesses operate on the ground.
